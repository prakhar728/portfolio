Architected GasDock, a cross-chain Account Abstraction SDK enabling gasless transactions where users pay fees in ERC-20 tokens on a different chain than where they execute (e.g., execute on Polygon, pay in ERC-20 on Base).

Designed relay server and escrow-based settlement architecture. Implemented security primitives: IntentHash binding, Permit2 witness validation, escrow-based refunds for failed transactions, and Chainlink-based gas-to-token price conversion.

Delivered client SDK, relay server, Foundry-based smart contracts, deployment scripts, and multi-chain testnet documentation.
