Built a decentralized healthcare platform connecting patients and doctors via blockchain, built for the Web3 BUILD Hackathon in partnership with NEAR Horizon.

Patients maintain full control over their medical data stored on-chain. Secure, transparent patient-doctor connections without centralized data custodians.

Led a 3-member team; co-authored Rust smart contracts compiled to WASM and integrated the NEAR frontend. Selected to showcase at NearCon'23, Portugal.
